# Protocol: Morning Briefing

## Triggers
"morning briefing", "good morning", "brief me", "what's today", "what do I have today"

## Steps
1. Get today's events from Google Calendar (user's primary calendar).
2. Search Gmail INBOX for unread/important threads since yesterday morning.
3. Read all `todos/*.md` for: items due today, overdue items, top high-priority pending.
4. Read `memory/short-term/` for active context.
5. Compose briefing in this order:
   - **Calendar today** — events with times, chronological
   - **Inbox highlights** — count + 2-3 items needing attention
   - **Due today** — todos with today's due date
   - **Watch-list** — overdue or top high-priority items
   - **Suggested focus** — 1-2 priorities for the day

## Format
Bullet-style, scannable. Under 200 words unless the day is unusually packed.

## After briefing
Ask: "Anything to add to the day or capture before you start?"
