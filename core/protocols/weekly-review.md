# Protocol: Weekly Review

## Triggers
"weekly review", "let's review the week", "Sunday review"

## Steps
1. **Last week recap**
   - Completed todos in the past 7 days (across all categories)
   - Calendar events that happened
2. **Backlog health**
   - Todos older than 14 days untouched → flag as stale, ask to keep/drop/defer
   - Overdue items → re-prioritize
3. **Financial check** (mini version of monthly check-in)
   - Recent receipts/orders pulled from Gmail
   - Any charges that look anomalous
   - Phase progress from `memory/short-term/financial-plan-status.md`
4. **Goal check-ins**
   - Read `profile/goals.md`, prompt the user for progress on active goals
5. **Look ahead**
   - Next 7 days of calendar
   - Prep needed for any meetings/events
6. **Decisions waiting** — anything in `decisions/` marked pending input?
7. **Inbox sweep** — review `inbox/inbox.md`, process entries into todos/memory/skills

## Output
Section-by-section summary. End with: "Anything to capture or commit to before next week?"
